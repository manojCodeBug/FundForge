The Vision

Project Verdant is a pioneering initiative to merge urban living with large-scale ecosystem restoration. We are designing a self-sustaining vertical forest that houses 5,000 residents while generating its own clean energy and managing 100% of its waste.

By leveraging the Stellar network for transparent, cross-border funding, we are ensuring that every XLM contributed is tracked directly to structural milestones. This isn't just a building; it's a blueprint for the future of decentralized urbanism.
